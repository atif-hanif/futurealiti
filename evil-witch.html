Concept of Character Design

After much effort, our team found a unique character idea covering the horror feel. It was crucial to converting ideas into reality, especially when it comes to character design from scratch. But our expert designers' record proves that they can make the impossible possible. Because of the team's great effort and professional approach, a great memorable character named Evil witch, which covers a half-human body and half spider, became a reality.

Who is the Evil Witch?

During the brainstorming for the character, it was decided that this character mode, feel, and features must communicate through visuals. The evil witch has had to be powerful and visually horror, having evil power and bad-tempered at the same time. We were confident that this character would be the best horror character that attracted viewers.

Sketches Of Evil Witch

After brainstorming and the concept of the character, real work had to start that was Sketch of the evil witch.We have to work on the main Sketch of the evil witch due to our designer's hard work and great effort. This work was done before the time.

After this part, we had to work on this character sketch covering its movement and acting and different poses. These poses include how the witch will look when it looks on the left side and right side, and sketches also cover different movements of the legs.

Concept of Evil Witch

The concept of evil covers all its specifications like a character can through the web because this character is a mixture of human and spider with this character should look dangerous and bad-tempered because the character's role is villain having some extraordinary power.

Ready Design

After sketch finalization, It had been a long way, but we were done it fast. The team started work to make the character its natural form, and within a couple of days, all things that needed to be done evil were ready as per expectation, which is provided in the sketch phase and all emotions designs.
