The Team For Creating Your Ideal Character Design

We have the following experts on board who would be glad to create perfect character design for you.

# Art Director

A seasoned artist with vast experience in creating game assets, art lead directs his/her colleagues from the very first steps. This person makes sure that each and every team member involved in the project is on the same page with the Client’s vision on a regular basis. Thus art lead is responsible for effectively conveying the Client vision into 2D art created by the entire project team.

# Lead 2D/3D Character Design

Grizzled 2D/3D artists that know how to deliver in the most time-effective manner. They do the heavy lifting for the entire 2D/3D art team by taking on the most challenging tasks. Normally it is a senior artist who creates the first characters that serve as the benchmark for the entire project.

# Project Manager

Project manager bridges the gap between the Client and the project team. This person ensures that the team runs smoothly and keeps the big picture in mind. Seasoned Project Manager can effectively lead large teams by keeping every member in line with the Client’s goals.

# 2D/3D Artists

There are different directions in 2D/3D art. And of course, there are a plethora of design styles. We have artists that excel in various 2D/3D character modelling directions: cartoony, realistic, stylized etc. Rest assured, we will tailor the team for your specific needs.

# Concept Artists

Concept artists lay the foundation for the entire art team, including 2D character designers. They are the first to translate Client’s vision into eye-catching concept art. Just like 2D game art experts, concept artists often excel in specific art niches and styles.
