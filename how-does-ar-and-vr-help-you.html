What is AR / VR?

First of all, you need to know the full forms of AR and VR, and then we'll give you a quick intro to both of them.

  • AR (Augmented Reality)
  • VR (Virtual Reality)

Virtual Reality is a technology that completely transforms the world into a virtual world according to people's requirements. In Virtual Reality, you'll experience a completely different world where everything will be virtual but looks like a real thing; on the other hand, AR enhances your experience by staying in the real world with the help of visualization like images, sound and text.

Difference Between AR and VR

Augmented Reality Virtual Reality
AR adds (augments) to the real-world scene. VR completely replaces what people see and experience.
Augmented reality is open and only partially immersive. In a virtual reality experience, users are fully immersed.
In a virtual reality experience, users are fully immersed. Enabled through VR headsets such as google daydream, Oculus Go, Google Cardboard or Samsung Gear VR.
AR Samples: Pokemon GO, Snapchat lenses VR Samples: Smallworlds and Second life
