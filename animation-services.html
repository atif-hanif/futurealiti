Our Video Animation Work Process

We partner up with the clients to understand their business and create a video animation symbolic of the company and brand. See how we make high-quality animated videos for you.

1

Discovery

The video animation project begins with a discovery mission. First, we ask you some questions about your company, your target audience, what inspires your consumers, and the message you want to deliver.

2

Script & Storyboard

We begin our video animation concepts with a solid narrative and storyboard. The script guides the visuals in your video and expresses the story you want to tell the consumer. We start by listening to the script and creating scenes and characters in the client's voice.

3

Voice Over

We produce audio by combining music, sound effects, and our vocal talent. Our voice-over crew will transform your screenplay and storyboard into a compelling animated video to assist you in educating and attracting more clients.

4

Illustrations

Prototyping is a critical phase in the video animation process. We create high-quality illustrations in our video animation work process that serve as a pattern for our video production team to follow. It enables them to make changes and assure overall video integrity.

5

Production

We produce unique, professional-quality video animations from scratch using professional graphics tools and industry-standard technology. Our primary goal is to create the most fantastic video possible.

6

Delivery

We are ready to provide you after you have approved the overall final production audio, visuals, and animations. We export all of the videos in multiple formats so that clients can use them on their websites right away.
